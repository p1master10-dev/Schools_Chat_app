Kwitter 2 ( Information )



  1. This App is made for students and teachers to communicate to each other
  2. They can use this app for different Events , Homeworks , Schools Closing etc.
  3. Just Sign Up With your name and start joining or creating rooms

    Steps To Begin

  • Write your name in the input box
  • Click on sign up
  • You will be taken to the main menu screen
  • Create Your own room
  • Before that see the option as "Mark As Teachers Room" if you are a teacher then click on it
  • And Create

  • Or You can join Others room
